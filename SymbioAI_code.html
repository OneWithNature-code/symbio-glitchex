How symbiosis works

Four intelligent steps transform urban waste streams into regenerative resource loops—automatically, continuously, and at city scale.

01
📡
Declare Resources
Businesses and institutions log their excess outputs—surplus heat, food, water, or materials—through our simple onboarding interface.
02
🤖
AI Matching
Our engine analyzes output profiles, proximity, timing, and demand across the entire urban network to find optimal exchange pairings.
03
🤝
Smart Exchange
Matched parties receive connection proposals with logistics, pricing suggestions, and compliance checks—ready to act in one click.
04
📊
Track Impact
Real-time dashboards show CO₂ avoided, cost savings, and circular economy contribution scores across every exchange completed.
